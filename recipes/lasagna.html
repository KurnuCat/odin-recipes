Lasagna

Description:

Delicious cheesy lasagna

Ingredients:

  • 1 liter of milk
  • 1 deciliter of wheat flour
  • 150 grams of grated cheese
  • Butter (a pinch of salt and white pepper)
  • 400 grams of ground meat
  • 2 onions
  • 400 grams of crushed tomatoes
  • A splash of cream
  • Pepper mix, grilling seasoning, garlic, oregano
  • 100 grams of grated cheese
  • Additionally Lasagna sheets

Steps:

  1. Cheese Sauce:

    2. Melt a generous knob of butter (oil can also be used) in a non-stick pot and add the flour (do not brown it). Gradually add the milk. Let it simmer for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove the pot from the heat and add the grated cheese and seasonings.

  2. Ground Meat Sauce:

    3. Brown the ground meat and onion in a pan. Add the crushed tomatoes, cream, and seasonings, and let it simmer for a moment.

  3. Assemble the Lasagna:

    4. Place half of the ground meat sauce at the bottom of a baking dish, followed by lasagna sheets, then cheese sauce, and another layer of lasagna sheets. Add the remaining ground meat sauce to the dish, followed by one more layer of lasagna sheets, and top with the remaining cheese sauce. Sprinkle grated cheese on top. Bake in the middle of the oven at 175 degrees Celsius (about 350 degrees Fahrenheit) for approximately 45 minutes or until the top is beautifully browned.
