Pizza

Description:

Delicious pizza base with fillings of your choice

Ingredients:

  • ½ pack dry yeast
  • 2 dl water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • 5 dl wheat flour

Steps:

  1. Mix the yeast into lukewarm water, add salt and oil.
  2. Mix in the wheat flour and if needed more than (5dl) so that the dough doesn't stick to your fingers.
  3. Let the dough rise in a bowl under a cloth for approx. 30 min.
  4. Roll out, fill and bake in a 225°C (435°F) oven for approx. 20 min.
  5. (Fillings of your choice)
