Pasta bolognese

Description:

Delicious pasta bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 170 g bacon
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 onions
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 400 grams of pork-beef minced meat
  • 370 g crushed tomatoes
  • 200 g pureed tomatoes
  • 70 g tomato paste
    • 1/2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp meat stock
  • 2 tbsp red balsamic vinegar
  • 200 ml red wine
  • black pepper (to taste)
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp dried basil
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 tsp dried thyme
  • pasta (tagliatelle or penne)
  • grated parmesan (for serving)
  • fresh basil (for garnish)

Steps:

  1. Dice the bacon, peel and finely chop the onion and garlic, and peel and cube the carrots.
  2. Sauté the bacon in a hot pan, then add the minced meat.
  3. Cook the minced meat until browned. Add the carrots, onion, and garlic, then reduce the heat to medium and sauté for a few minutes.
  4. Add the remaining ingredients and let simmer covered on low heat for about 45 minutes. The flavor of the sauce will improve with a longer simmering time.
  5. Cook the pasta al dente and mix it into the sauce. Serve with chopped fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese.
